Event Promotion and Community Engagement

October 22nd, 6:00-7:00 PM Central Time

Session Objective

Train VPs of PR to promote club events (e.g., open houses, speech contests) and build community partnerships.

Key Topics

  • Creating an event promotion plan: timeline, channels, and target audiences
  • Leveraging free community calendars and local media
  • Building relationships with local organizations for co-hosted events
  • Measuring event success and gathering feedback

Agenda (60 minutes)

  • 5 min: Importance of event promotion
  • 15 min: Case study: Successful club event promotion
  • 20 min: Group activity: Plan a promotional campaign
  • 15 min: Share plans and discuss challenges
  • 5 min: Q&A and action items

Why It's Valuable

Events drive member recruitment, but VPs of PR often lack a structured promotion approach. This session provides a clear framework.

Preparation

Think about upcoming events your club would like to promote.
