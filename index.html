About the Training

This four-part monthly training series is designed specifically for Club Vice Presidents of Public Relations in District 106. Each one-hour session focuses on key PR competencies to help you promote your Toastmasters club effectively, enhance visibility, and engage both members and the community.

All sessions will be held from 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM Central Time on the following Wednesdays:

  • August 20th
  • September 24th
  • October 22nd
  • November 12th
