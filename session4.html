Measuring PR Success and Continuous Improvement

November 12th, 6:00-7:00 PM Central Time

Session Objective

Teach VPs of PR to assess PR efforts and plan for ongoing improvement.

Key Topics

  • Key performance indicators (KPIs) for PR efforts
  • Using free analytics tools to track performance
  • Gathering feedback from members and guests
  • Creating a 90-day PR plan aligned with club goals

Agenda (60 minutes)

  • 5 min: Why measuring PR impact matters
  • 15 min: Overview of analytics tools and interpreting data
  • 20 min: Workshop: Develop a simple 90-day PR plan
  • 15 min: Share plans and discuss adjustments
  • 5 min: Q&A and action items

Why It's Valuable

VPs of PR often focus on tasks without evaluating impact. This session helps them measure success and plan strategically.

Preparation

Bring any metrics or data you currently track for your club's PR efforts.
