Crafting Compelling PR Content

September 24th, 6:00-7:00 PM Central Time

Session Objective

Teach VPs of PR to create engaging content (newsletters, press releases, social posts) to promote club events and successes.

Key Topics

  • Writing effective press releases and media pitches for local outlets
  • Creating a monthly newsletter: structure, content ideas, and tools
  • Storytelling techniques to highlight club achievements
  • Using visuals (photos, videos, infographics) to enhance content appeal

Agenda (60 minutes)

  • 5 min: Importance of compelling content
  • 15 min: Breakdown of sample press release and newsletter
  • 20 min: Workshop: Draft a press release or newsletter section
  • 15 min: Peer review and feedback
  • 5 min: Q&A and action items

Why It's Valuable

VPs of PR often need guidance on creating professional content. This session provides templates and practice to build confidence.

Preparation

Bring examples of PR content your club has used in the past (if available).
