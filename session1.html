Building a Strong Club Brand and Online Presence

August 20th, 6:00-7:00 PM Central Time

Session Objective

Equip VPs of PR with strategies to create a consistent and professional club brand across digital platforms.

Key Topics

  • Understanding Toastmasters' branding guidelines (logos, colors, fonts)
  • Creating and optimizing a club website using tools like FreeToastHost, Wix, or WordPress
  • Setting up and managing social media accounts (Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram)
  • Best practices for posting: frequency, tone, and content aligned with Toastmasters' mission

Agenda (60 minutes)

  • 5 min: Introduction to branding importance
  • 15 min: Overview of Toastmasters branding resources and examples
  • 20 min: Hands-on exercise: Draft a sample social media post
  • 15 min: Group discussion: Share ideas for improving club's online presence
  • 5 min: Q&A and action items

Why It's Valuable

A consistent brand builds credibility and attracts members. This session addresses common issues with outdated or inconsistent online presence.

Preparation

Please review your club's current website and social media profiles before the session.
